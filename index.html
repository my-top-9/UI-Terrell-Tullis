About Top Nine

Do you remember MySpace Top 8 friends ? Well this is MyTopNine - a website that lets users select their Top-Nine in a variety of categories (sports, video games, makeup, movies, etc..). My Top-Nine is a much easier way to communitcate, it defines you as a person with your interests which helps breakdown those awkward conversation starters.
